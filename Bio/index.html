Jason Aldean

The Guy that sings Big Green Tractor

Jason Aldean is a country rock star who was born on February 28,1977 in Macon, Georgia. Even since his youth he has wanted to perform on stage. At age 14 he began to perform, with his mothers help, he peformed at talent contests and local fairs. When he turned 15 he joined a house band and also changed his name from Aldine to Aldean. After graduating highschool the band and him bagen touring at clubs and festivals. Through 2000 through 2010 he began dropping albums such as "Hicktown", Relentless, and finally Wide Open where he dropped the hit song of the century. Big Green Tractor. This song changed my life and also the entire worlds. Of his singles 19 have reached number one on Hot Country Songs or Counrty Airpay charts. Eight more of his singles have reached the top 10.

His hit song Big Green Tractor changed the lives of millions.
